About Us

Logbookonline has been developed to allow the consumer, the opportunity to track your vehicles mileage and ultimately save you money via accurate tax returns. We have simplified the product by allowing both manual and GPS tracking via Mobile applications available across Android, Iphone and Blackberry.

The site along with all your data logs is also viewable and backed up via the web and mobisites accessible on www.logbookonline.co.za. Maintaining an accurate logbook could be a tedious affair. This application insures ease of use which results in additional tax savings. You can now base your claims for travel expenses, both private and business, on the actual distance travelled.

Furthermore we have included back end development to save you more money by informing you, if and when your vehicle is over insured, based on your vehicles depreciation.

Opting into this is all part of the service and available to all users on registration. Its a known fact that R100's of Millions of Rands are wasted each year based on over insured vehicles. With over insurance you are at risk of paying too much in premiums from the moment the market value of the insured vehicle is less than the amount insured

Logbookonline is the go too App to insure savings all around!
